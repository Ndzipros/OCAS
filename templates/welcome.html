How It Works

Three simple steps to campus-wide safety

OCAS is designed to be simple for both administrators and students or staff on campus.

01
Create an Account
Students and staff sign up with their name and email. Once registered, they connect to the campus network and access the live alert feed from any device.
02
Connect to the Network
Open the OCAS feed page on any phone, laptop, or tablet connected to the campus Wi-Fi or LAN. Your device is now registered and receiving alerts.
03
Receive Alerts Instantly
When an admin broadcasts an alert, every connected device sees it within seconds and plays a sound. No app install needed — it works directly in the browser.
Signal Flow

How alerts reach every device

Admin sends an alert

The administrator logs into the OCAS control panel and broadcasts a new alert with a title, message, and priority level.

Alert is saved on the server

The alert is instantly written to the local campus server database — no internet connection is required at any point.

All devices detect the new alert

Every connected device polls the server every 5 seconds. When a new alert is detected, it appears on screen immediately.

Sound notification plays

A sound alert plays automatically on every device so users are notified even if they are not actively watching the screen.
